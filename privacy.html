Privacy Policy

Plain English version: we collect almost nothing, your notes stay on your device, and the AI sorting only happens when you ask it to.

Last updated: July 20, 2026

The short version

  • Your written notes and reminders are stored on your device, not on our servers.
  • The AI sorting (figuring out if something is a note or a reminder) sends your text to a third-party AI provider only when you submit a thought. It is not analyzed in the background.
  • If you join the waitlist, we store your email. That's it. No name, no profile, no tracking.
  • We don't run analytics, ad pixels, or third-party trackers on this site.
  • We don't sell, share, or rent your data. Ever.

1. What we collect

Email address (optional). If you join the waitlist, your email is stored in a Cloudflare KV namespace accessible only to the project maintainer. It's used to notify you when Monolog launches on iOS. You can request removal at any time by emailing timppamsix@gmail.com.

Notes and reminders (your content). These are saved locally on your device (browser storage for the web app, device storage for the iOS app). They are never sent to our servers unless the AI sorting feature is invoked.

Payment information. When paid plans launch, payments will be processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number — that's handled entirely by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure.

2. How the AI sorting works

When you submit a thought for sorting, the text of that thought is sent over HTTPS to Groq (the AI inference provider), which processes it and returns a structured response (note vs. reminder, scheduled time if applicable). The result comes back to your device.

What this means:

  • Only the specific thought you submit is sent — not your entire notes library.
  • The transfer is encrypted in transit.
  • How long Groq retains that data is governed by Groq's privacy policy. We don't control their retention.
  • If you don't submit a thought, nothing leaves your device.

3. Cookies and tracking

We don't use cookies for tracking. We don't run Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party analytics. We don't fingerprint you. If you clear your browser storage, your notes go with it — we have no copy.

4. Third-party services

  • Cloudflare — hosts the worker that powers AI sorting and the waitlist. Their privacy policy applies to infrastructure-level data.
  • Groq — provides the AI model that classifies your thoughts.
  • Vercel — hosts this website.
  • Stripe (future) — handles payments for paid plans.

5. Data retention

Notes and reminders: Stored on your device until you delete them or clear your browser/app storage. We have no server-side copy.

Waitlist emails: Kept until you request removal or until the iOS launch emails have been sent. After that, you can ask us to delete your email.

6. Your rights

You can:

  • Access the data we hold about you (just your waitlist email, if you joined).
  • Delete it by emailing us.
  • Export your notes from the app at any time (copy/paste or future export feature).

To exercise any of these, email timppamsix@gmail.com from the address you signed up with.

7. Children's privacy

Monolog is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has given us their email, contact us and we'll delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle data, we'll update this page and bump the "Last updated" date. For material changes (e.g., new third-party processor, new data type collected), we'll also notify waitlist members by email before the change takes effect.

9. Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns? Email timppamsix@gmail.com. Response time is usually within a few days.

How it works

One, two, three.

You write. Monolog understands. The right thing happens. You're done.

  1. 01

    Type your thought

    Write it the way you'd say it out loud. No menus, no structure — just the words as they come.

    “buy milk saturday” · “call mom tomorrow” · “the meeting went well”
  2. 02

    Monolog figures it out

    Is this a note to keep, or a reminder that needs a time? It reads your words and understands — no extra steps from you.

    a reminder for saturday · a note about your day · it just knows
  3. 03

    Saved or fired

    It lands as a note you can read later, or pops up as a reminder at exactly the right moment.

    works offline · stays on your phone · you did nothing but write
